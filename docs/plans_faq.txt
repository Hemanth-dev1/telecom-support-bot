TELECOM PLANS - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What plans are available?
A: We offer three plans: Basic Plan (Rs.199/month, 20GB), Unlimited Pro (Rs.599/month, 100GB), and Family Pack (Rs.999/month, 150GB shared).

Q: What happens when I exceed my data limit?
A: Your speed is throttled to 1Mbps after you exceed your plan limit. You can purchase a 10GB add-on pack for Rs.99.

Q: Can I change my plan mid-cycle?
A: Yes. Plan changes take effect from the next billing cycle. There is no fee for upgrades. Downgrades require 3 days notice.

Q: What is the validity of each plan?
A: All plans are valid for 30 days from activation or renewal date.

Q: Is there a contract or lock-in period?
A: No. All plans are month-to-month with no lock-in period.
