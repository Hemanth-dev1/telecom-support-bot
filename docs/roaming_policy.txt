INTERNATIONAL ROAMING POLICY

Eligible Plans: Roaming is available on Unlimited Pro and Family Pack plans only.
Basic Plan does not include roaming benefits.

Included Countries (Unlimited Pro):
USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Australia
- Data: 1GB/day at local speeds, then 128Kbps
- Calls: Rs.5/minute incoming, Rs.10/minute outgoing
- SMS: Rs.2/SMS outgoing

How to activate: Dial *123# and select option 3, or activate via the app before travel.

Fair Usage Policy: Roaming data is subject to a 30GB monthly cap.

Roaming Charges after FUP: Rs.30 per GB or Rs.2 per minute calls.

Important: Disable roaming in phone settings when not abroad to avoid accidental charges.
