REFUND POLICY

Eligibility: Refund requests must be raised within 7 days of payment.

Full Refund: Available if service was not activated or activated less than 24 hours ago.

Partial Refund: Calculated as unused days divided by 30, multiplied by plan price.
Example: Cancel on day 10 of a 30-day Rs.599 plan: refund = (20/30) x 599 = Rs.399

Non-refundable items:
- Add-on data packs once activated
- International roaming charges incurred
- Plans renewed within 48 hours of renewal date

How to request a refund:
1. Contact support via app or website
2. Provide your mobile number and transaction ID
3. Refund processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method

Escalation: If not resolved in 7 days, escalate to nodal officer at nodal@telecom.example.com
